◆ AESNDVoiceFormat

enum struct ogcwrap::aesnd::AESNDVoiceFormat : u8
strong

Enum to specify the format of PCM samples that the DSP will use.

Enumerator
MonoU8 

Specifies that the PCM samples only have one sound channel (mono) and that they have unsigned 8-bit values.

MonoS8 

Specifies that the PCM samples are in mono and that they have signed 8-bit values.

MonoU16 

Specifies that the PCM samples are in mono and that they have unsigned 16-bit values.

MonoS16 

Specifies that the PCM samples are in mono and that they have signed 16-bit values.

StereoU8 

Specifies that the PCM samples have two sound channels (stereo) and that they have unsigned 8-bit values.

StereoS8 

Specifies that the PCM samples are in stereo and that they have signed 8-bit values.

StereoU16 

Specifies that the PCM samples are in stereo and that they have unsigned 16-bit values.

StereoS16 

Specifies that the PCM samples are in stereo and that they have signed 16-bit values.

Note
The frequency does not necessarily have to be VOICE_FREQ32KHZ, VOICE_FREQ48KHZ, or DSP_DEFAULT_FREQ; it could be any frequency. However, it is recommended that the PCM samples be in one of these formats to ensure compatibility.
